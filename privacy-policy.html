1. Overview

CodexPts helps PUBG Mobile players and squads track match statistics, manage rosters, use recruitment tools, browse scrims organizer listings, and register for tournaments. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Information we collect

Account & profile

Email, display name, in-game name (IGN), country, squad membership, roles, optional avatar, and account type (player or scrims organizer).

Squad & recruitment

Squad name, tag, logo, roster, recruiting settings, join requests, and optional application details (availability, device, experience, social handles).

Stats & media

Player card data, match statistics, match screenshots you upload, weekly reports, and map strategy plans.

Scrims & tournaments

Organizer brand info, verifications, tournament listings, and registration applications.

Device hash (moderation)

A SHA-256 hash derived from device identifiers (not raw IDs) used for ban enforcement and sign-in checks — not for ads.

Local storage

Cached data on your device for offline use.

3. Photos & on-device OCR

With permission, you select photos from your gallery for match screenshots, player cards, and avatars/logos. We do not scan your entire library.

Text recognition (Google ML Kit) runs on your device to parse stats and validate screenshots. Confirmed stats and images may sync to our cloud. The app uses gallery pickers only; camera is not used in the current version.

4. Cloud storage (Supabase)

Signed-in users sync data to Supabase (database + storage for avatars, logos, cards, screenshots). Row-level security limits access. See Supabase privacy.

5. How we use data

To operate the app, sync squads, show recruitment/tournaments, generate stats, moderate abuse, and improve reliability. We do not sell personal information.

6. Sharing

With your squad, recruitment/tournament counterparties, scrims organizers (when you interact), moderators, service providers (Supabase), and when required by law.

7. Deletion

Delete your account in Settings → Delete account. Contact us if deletion fails.

8. Ads & subscriptions

Current release: No ads or in-app purchases active (“Coming soon”). Future AdMob/Play Billing will be disclosed before launch.

9. Children

Not directed at children under 13. Contact us to request removal of a child’s data.
