How to delete your account (in the app)

  1. Open CodexPts on your Android device.
  2. Sign in with the account you want to remove.
  3. Go to Settings (account screen).
  4. Scroll to Delete account and confirm in the dialog.
  5. Stay online until deletion finishes — the app will sign you out when done.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone from the app.

What is deleted

  • Your cloud profile and sign-in account
  • Your player card and personal profile data stored for your account
  • Your membership link to squads (you are removed from team rosters)

What may remain

  • Match history or screenshots already stored under a squad may still be visible to that squad until removed by team staff
  • Moderation or security records we must keep for abuse prevention (where allowed by law)

If deletion fails

Email codexesports70@gmail.com from the same address you used to register, with your in-game name (IGN). We will delete your account manually within a reasonable time.
